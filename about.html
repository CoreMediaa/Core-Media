About Us

Core Media is a digital marketing and creative communication agency, pioneering integrated solutions that enhance brand value and build strong, lasting connections between businesses and their target audiences. We believe every brand has a story worth telling — impressively, emotionally, and persuasively.

Key Service Areas

With a dynamic team of experienced professionals and forward-thinking minds, Core Media focuses on five key service areas:

  • – Strategic digital marketing solutions across multiple platforms
  • – Brand identity design that ensures consistency, professionalism, and uniqueness
  • – Creative content production, from videos and motion graphics to visual storytelling
  • – UI/UX design for websites, apps, and interactive digital experiences
  • – Integrated communication campaigns, combining creativity with measurable impact

Vision

By 2030, Core Media aims to become one of Vietnam’s top 3 leading digital marketing and creative agencies, on par with major media corporations in the region. We strive not only to lead in delivering professional, world-class marketing solutions, but also to pioneer innovative approaches that connect Vietnamese brands to global audiences. Core Media will become a symbol of breakthrough creativity, strategic excellence, and a trusted partner for brands seeking meaningful digital impact.

Mission

With intelligence, creativity, passion, and a strong commitment to values, Core Media accompanies businesses in crafting impactful marketing experiences that resonate deeply with their communities. We embrace challenges, constantly innovate, and are committed to delivering every campaign with artistic, cultural, and strategic excellence. Beyond business success, we carry a mission to build lasting cultural and brand values — ensuring every project we create becomes part of a modern marketing legacy with human connection at its core.
